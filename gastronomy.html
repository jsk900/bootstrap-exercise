Churros

A churro is a fried-dough pastry—predominantly choux—based snack. Churros are traditional in Spain and Portugal, from where they originate, as well as the Philippines and Ibero-America.

Cocido madrileño

is a traditional chickpea-based stew from Madrid, Spain. A substantial dish prepared with meat and vegetables, it is most popular during the winter but is served throughout the year in some restaurants.

Lentejas

is a traditional lentil based stew originally from the province of Avila. Served with potatoes, carrots, Chorizo and Morcilla (Black pudding) All ingredientes apart from the lentils are optional.

Paella

is a Valencian rice dish that has ancient roots but its modern form originated in the mid-19th century in the area around Albufera lagoon on the east coast of Spain adjacent to the city of Valencia. There are many different variations of this classic dish.
